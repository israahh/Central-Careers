About Central Careers

Central Careers is Central High School's official job and career connection platform desgined to help students explore real-world opportunties beyond the classroom. Central Careers connects you with local employers who are ready to invest in your potential. Every job listing is reviewed by our guidance department to ensure it's safe and beneficcial for our students.

Our Mission

To empower Central Indians with tools and opportunties that prepare them for life after high school-one application at a time.

Tips for Students

Here are some tips to help you succeed in your job search:

  • Keep your resume updated and tailored to the job you're applying for.
  • Practice common interview questions to build confidence.
  • Network with professionals in your field of interest.
  • Take advantage of internships and volunteer opportunities to gain experience.
